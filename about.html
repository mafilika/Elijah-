About Us

Professional Glass & Aluminium Supply and Installation for Residential, Commercial & Industrial Projects

Bilastos Trading and Projects specializes in supplying and installing high-quality glass and aluminium systems. We deliver precision-engineered solutions for windows, doors, shower doors, balustrades, and custom aluminium framing. Our team is qualified, professional, and committed to providing durable, safe, and aesthetically superior installations.

We serve Residential, Commercial, and Industrial clients, ensuring every project is completed on time, on budget, and to the highest standards. Our mission is to combine quality, compliance, and innovation to position ourselves as leaders in the Glass & Aluminium industry.
